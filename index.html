Need For Speed Underground 2

October 2, 2023 by G. Lawrence

Need for Speed Underground 2 is a popular racing video game released in 2004, developed by EA Black Box and published by Electronic Arts. It's the sequel to "Need for Speed: Underground" and is part of the "Need for Speed" series. The game is set in an open-world city environment and focuses on illegal street racing and customization of vehicles.

Firstly, Underground 2 is known for its extensive vehicle customization options. Players can modify and personalize their cars with various visual upgrades, including body kits, rims, vinyl graphics, and more. This aspect adds a layer of depth and personalization to the gameplay. The game features an open-world environment, the fictional city of Bayview. Players can freely explore the city, discover hidden races, and find shops for car upgrades. The open-world design offers a sense of freedom and exploration. The game offers a variety of race modes, including circuit races, sprint races, drag races, and drift races. This diversity keeps the gameplay engaging, and players can choose their preferred racing style. And Last but not least, Underground 2 features a memorable and diverse soundtrack with tracks from various genres (mostly rock & roll and rap music) enhancing the overall gaming experience.

If you enjoy customizing cars and street racing, this game is worth checking out for its nostalgic and immersive gameplay.

Dragonball FighterZ

October 1, 2023 by G. Lawrence

Dragon Ball FighterZ is a 2D fighting game developed by Arc System Works and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It's part of the Dragon Ball franchise and has gained considerable attention for its stunning visuals and gameplay faithful to the anime series.

Dragon Ball FighterZ has a single-player story mode that includes an original story featuring an army of android duplicates and a new character, Android 21. The game is widely praised for capturing the look and feel of the Dragon Ball anime with beautifully animated characters and vibrant, high-energy battles. The game features a diverse roster of Dragon Ball characters, each with unique moves and abilities. Whether you're a fan of the Z Fighters or the series' many villains, you'll find your favorite characters to play as.
